Good Old Fashioned Pancakes

Description

Fluffy pancakes perfect for weekend breakfasts.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp butter, melted

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter. Mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Scoop the batter onto the griddle, approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
